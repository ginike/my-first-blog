Time Signature

The upper part of the time signature

The top number in a time signature indicates how many beats wil be present in a measure. The bottom number in the time signature indicates what kind of noe is considered one beat.

Example

4/4 Time signature

The top 4 says there are four beats in a measure(have exceptions. The lower 4 is the numerical representation of a Crotcet. This means every crotchet in the measure would be given one beat.)
